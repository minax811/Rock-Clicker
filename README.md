# Rock-Clicker
Its a cookie clicker type of game made in html instead of clicking cookies you click a rock and get shards, use those shards to get upgrades. The currency used to buy upgrades in this game is shards. basically 1 damage point is equal to 1 shard and getting multipliers will allow you to get more then 1 damage per click thus also increaisng the rate at which you get shards
# Objective of the game
You have to break the rock and reach the gem. A rock has 10,000 health. 1 damage decreases 1 health. Initally one click will do 1 damage only however with the help of multipliers you can increase it to 3x,6x,12x,25x mutliplier meaning each click will do X amount of damage. Another feature thats in the game is Nuclear Decay using 500 shards you can buy a Nuclear decay that decays health at a constant rate
# Game Features
* Multipliers
* Nuclear decay
* hits Counter
* Rock Health
* Shards Counter
* Upgrades Bar
* Damage per click Text
* Homepage/main menu button
* sound effects
* sound ON and OFF button
* rock sharters when its health reaches 0
